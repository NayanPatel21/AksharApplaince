Oven Installation Sydney

While buying a new oven, getting it professionally installed is an important thing to consider. Our professional technicians make the installation easier for you. We install your oven by working on the following protocol procedure:

  • Our technician evaluate your kitchen and ensure that all the connections are in order and meet the standard requirements
  • Then they un-box the new oven and check it thoroughly
  • If you want to install a gas oven, the technician would connect it to an existing gas pipe and test for leaks
